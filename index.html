taste

Meaning a gustatory sensation of flavor perceived in the mouth and throat on contact with food. It can also be defined as a sensation obtained from experiencing food, typically produced by the stimulation of the sense of taste combined with those of touch and smell

texture

Food texture is defined as those properties of a foodthat are sensed by touch in the mouth and with the hands. We use many words to describe food texture—foods can be soft or hard, mushy or crunchy, or smooth or lumpy. Texture is important to the enjoyment and acceptability of foods.

balance of flavor

Meaning that one flavor counteracts or offsets that another to achieve an even more harmonious taste. Ex.: spice balances sweet and sweet balances spice. ...

depth of flavor

Depth of flavor refers to the layers of flavor within a dish, or it's complexity. Using various techniques a chef can manipulate the flavor of a dish.
